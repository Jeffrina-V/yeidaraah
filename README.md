# yeidaraah
AI Powered Food Waste Reduction And Food Redistribution
YEIDARAAH – AI Food Rescue Platform
🍽️ Overview
YEIDARAAH is an AI-powered food redistribution platform developed by Team Manna Hackers to reduce food waste and support hunger relief.
The platform connects Hotels, NGOs, and Volunteers to ensure surplus food is quickly identified, verified, and distributed to people in need.
By combining AI, real-time location tracking, and cloud databases, YEIDARAAH enables efficient food rescue operations.
🌍 Problem Statement
Every day, large quantities of edible food from restaurants, hotels, and events are wasted while millions of people face hunger.
Key challenges include:
Lack of coordination between food donors and NGOs
No real-time system for surplus food distribution
Inefficient logistics and tracking
💡 Solution
YEIDARAAH provides a smart digital platform where:
Hotels can report surplus food
NGOs receive instant notifications
Volunteers can collect and distribute food
AI helps optimize rescue operations
🚀 Key Features
🍛 Food Donation Posting
Hotels and restaurants can post surplus food details including quantity, location, and expiry time.
🤝 NGO Integration
Verified NGOs receive donation alerts and can accept requests.
📍 Live Location Tracking
Map integration helps NGOs locate nearby food donors.
🤖 AI Assistance
AI analyzes food data and suggests optimal distribution strategies.
☁️ Cloud Database
All data is securely stored using Firebase.
